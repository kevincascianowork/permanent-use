Permanent Use

Custodial land design for those who want more from life.

Most land mistakes aren’t aesthetic.
They’re functionally flawed, and useless.

Permanent Use exists to see second- and third-order consequences before anything is built—so the land remains coherent, durable, and usable long after initial intentions fade.

This is design-only work.
Finite scope. No implementation. No education. No open-ended process.

The Permanent Use Plan

A closed-scope land design and decision document that resolves long-term use conflicts before money, labor, or sentiment are committed.

  • Delivery: 14–21 days
  • Format: Remote, design-only
  • Intake: One recorded call (60–90 min)

Deliverables

  • Land-use structure
  • Constraint map
  • Phasing logic
  • Written tradeoff rationale
  • Final “do not cross” list

Explicit Exclusions

  • No planting plans
  • No species lists
  • No budgets
  • No coaching
  • No revisions beyond scope
  • No implementation support
